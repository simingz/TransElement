TransElement

Collaborated project at Gilad Lab and Stephens Lab.

Overview

The basic idea is firstly to get an overview of how the distribution of the H3K9me3 mark differs between the 2 species and secondly whether there are particular transposable elements that are silenced specifically in one species and try to get at a mechanism for this as transposable element silencing is a rapidly evolving phenomenon.
